FORWARD: Far-Out Reach to Worldwide Access and Retrieval of Data

Or, if you wish, Fairly Obsessed in the Romance of the Web And Relational Databases. The FORWARD group focuses on data access and retrieval, led byProfessor Kevin C.C. Chang at the Computer Science Department, the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. This site is FORWARD's public portal on our research, publications, people, demos, and datasets.
